Need Support?

If you are seeking support for complex mental health conditions, including bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, or psychotic depression, or if you are supporting a friend or family member facing these challenges, we’re here to help.

We will connect you to a fitting mental healthcare provider based on your situation, as well as provide financial aid.
