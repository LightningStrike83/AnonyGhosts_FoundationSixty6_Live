Who Are We?

We are Foundation Sixty6 and our mission is enhancing mental healthcare access for youth (16-25) with psychosis, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia in Southwestern Ontario.

We are here to support those experiencing significant barriers in accessing adequate mental healthcare for severe conditions that have recently been diagnosed under the umbrella of psychotic depression.
