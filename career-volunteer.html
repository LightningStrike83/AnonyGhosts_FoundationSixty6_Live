Careers and Volunteer Opportunities

WHY VOLUNTEER FOR US?

Volunteer with Foundation Sixty6 to directly impact youth mental health. Your commitment enhances access to mental healthcare for young individuals facing conditions like psychosis, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. Join us in creating a brighter, more resilient future for youth.

BUILD YOUR CAREER WITH US

Join Foundation Sixty6 for a purpose-driven career, shaping the future of youth mental health. Contribute to improved access to mental healthcare for ages 16-25, making a lasting impact on young minds. Your career here is a direct avenue for positive change in mental health.
